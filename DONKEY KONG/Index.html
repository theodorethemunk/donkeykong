About us

Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong Coin (DKC) is an exciting and vibrant cryptocurrency project that pays homage to the iconic character from the beloved Super Mario franchise.

Donkey Kong Coin seeks to create an engaging and immersive experience for both cryptocurrency enthusiasts and fans of the Super Mario universe. With its Super Mario-themed branding and a focus on community-driven initiatives, DKC aims to capture the excitement and joy that players have experienced throughout the years.

How to Buy

Step 1
Create Wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Step 2
Get Some Bnb

have BNB in your wallet to switch to $DKC. If you don’t have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Step 3
Go to Pancakeswap

connect to Pancakeswap. Go to app.Pancakeswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $DKC token address into Pancakeswap,and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Step 4
Swap BNB to Donkey Kong

switch BNB for $DKC. Slippage 8%
